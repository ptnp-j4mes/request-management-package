Interaction rules

  • Backdrops lock background scroll and close on click outside the surface.
  • Tab is trapped inside the active surface and focus returns to the trigger.
  • Enter submits the active form; Escape closes the modal or drawer.
  • Reduced motion removes springy entrance and progress animations.

Accessibility + production-ready behavior

Checks included in the demo implementation.

  • Focus is trapped inside the open surface and restored to the trigger after close.
  • Background scrolling is disabled while a dialog or drawer is active.
  • ARIA roles and labels are set per state, including alertdialog for destructive confirmation.
  • Loading, success, error, and destructive confirmation states are supported in real interactions.
  • Reduced-motion users see the same layout with minimal animation and no playful drift.
  • Mobile widths convert dialogs into bottom sheets and the drawer into a full-screen panel with a handle bar.
